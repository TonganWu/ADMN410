HISTORY

Durham House of Pets was founded in 2011 with the help of pets organization. It was formed with the help of a loan from this organization and soon grew to make its place in the market. Marked as one of the fastest growing markets in the country.

What We DO

We supply our clients with food for dog, cat and snake pets. Delivery was done in person on the early years of company startups but as time progressed Durham House of Pets has its own delivery channels to facilitate this.

We put your pet first at Durham House of Pets making it our business that your pet gets the best there is and that the supplies are of quality and healthy. We make free deliveries that exceed $24.99 to your doorstep.

Over the years we have grown through out the country, with a number of branches to deliver services faster and more efficiently to our clients. Durham House of Pets is planning to start making deliveries international as well as start branches in other countries.
